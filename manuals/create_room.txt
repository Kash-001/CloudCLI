Command: create_room

Usage:
  create_room <name> <datacenter_name>

Description:
  Creates a new room in a specified datacenter.

Parameters:
  - <name>: The name of the room.
  - <datacenter_name>: The name of the datacenter where the room will be created.

Example:
  create_room "Pegasus-Room" "Equinix-10"
